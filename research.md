Having completed my PhD at the Australian National University with a thesis on China’s urbanisation and domestic migration, I have since broadened my research interests to China’s engagements with the global South. Since 2017, I have taught anthropology and Chinese Studies at Sun Yat-sen University, the University of Botswana, and the Australian National University. My publications span science, technology, and society (STS), industrial relations, urbanisation, migration, and transnationalism in both global and local contexts.
